Subjective:
For about 3 months, he has been experiencing hard stools, sometimes with no bowel movements for 2-3 days. This is accompanied by abdominal pain and cramps. Feeling of bloating. Normal eating pattern, no change in appetite. Underweight, no weight loss. No blood in stools. Request for help: Wants to know if he should be concerned and how he can soften his stools.


Objective:
Abdomen: normal bowel sounds, varying tympany, tenderness in lower left abdomen, dullness in lower left abdomen.


Evaluation:
Irritable bowel syndrome


Plan:
Psyllium fibre sachets prescribed, to be taken daily with plenty of water for 6 weeks. Evaluation after 6 weeks. Safety net: contact us sooner if there is more pain, fever or blood in the stool. Discuss with dietitian next time.
